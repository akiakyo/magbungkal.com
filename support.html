How Support Works

Every ticket follows a clear review process.

01

Create a Ticket

Select the correct category and provide complete information.

02

Staff Review

An available team member reviews your concern and evidence.

03

Investigation

Staff verify logs, reports, transactions, or server behavior.

04

Resolution

You receive the result, instructions, or solution to your concern.

05

Ticket Closed

The ticket is archived after the concern has been resolved.

Before Opening a Ticket

Complete tickets are easier and faster for the team to process.

Include Your Username

Provide your exact Minecraft username, including capitalization and underscores.

Explain Clearly

Describe what happened, when it happened, and what result you expected.

Attach Evidence

Include screenshots, recordings, logs, or payment references when relevant.

Remain Respectful

Polite communication makes it easier for staff to understand and assist you.

One Issue Per Ticket

Avoid combining unrelated concerns into the same support ticket.
