Quick Rule Summary

The most important expectations every player should remember.

Do not cheat or exploit bugs.
Do not grief or steal from protected builds.
Respect SkyBlock islands, co-op members, and island permissions.
Respect all players and staff members.
Do not scam, duplicate, or manipulate the economy.
Keep chat appropriate and avoid spam.
Report serious violations through Discord tickets.

Possible Enforcement Actions

Actions depend on severity, evidence, history, intent, and impact.

01

Warning

For minor first-time violations.

02

Mute or Kick

For disruptive chat or repeated misconduct.

03

Temporary Ban

For serious or repeated violations.

04

Permanent Ban

For severe cheating, abuse, or major harm.

Staff may skip steps when an offense is severe. Punishment details are reviewed using available evidence.
